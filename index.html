Biography

Zhen Lu currently is a PhD candidate of Epidemiology and Biostatistics at the Sun Yat-sen University. He has made conributions to neurosyphilis and cancer research, and his work has been published in journals including eClinicalMedicine, The Lancet Global Health, JMIR Public Health Surveill, Journal of Medical Virology, The Lancet Regional Health ‑ Western Pacific, among others.

In addition to his work on neurosyphilis and cancer, Zhen Lu has extensive experience applying advanced statistical concepts, artificial intelligence, and causal inference methods to conduct real-world research based on electronic health records. He has conducted multiple studies in the areas of COVID-19, HPV infection, cardiovascular disease, and aging. To date (Nov 16, 2023), Zhen Lu has published 20 papers in peer-reviewed journals, serving as the first author and co-first author for 7 of these.
